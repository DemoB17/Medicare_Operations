# Insights and Recommendations

## Insight 1

Patients aged 18–59 account for the highest number of visits.

Recommendation

Allocate more staffing during peak adult clinic hours.

---

## Insight 2

Outstanding payments remain high.

Recommendation

Strengthen payment follow-up procedures.

---

## Insight 3

Certain doctors consistently receive higher satisfaction ratings.

Recommendation

Study their best practices and replicate them across departments.

---

## Insight 4

Long wait times correspond with lower patient satisfaction.

Recommendation

Review scheduling processes and optimize appointment allocation.

---

## Insight 5

A small number of diagnoses account for a large proportion of patient visits.

Recommendation

Ensure adequate staffing and medical resources for these high-demand conditions.
