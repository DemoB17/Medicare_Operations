# Business Problem

Healthcare organizations generate large volumes of operational data every day. Without proper analysis, it becomes difficult to identify performance bottlenecks, monitor financial health, and improve patient care.

This project seeks to answer questions such as:

- How many patients were served?
- Which age groups visit the hospital most?
- Which doctors handle the highest patient volume?
- How much revenue has been collected?
- How much payment remains outstanding?
- What is the average patient satisfaction score?
- Which diagnoses occur most frequently?
- Are appointment cancellations increasing?
