# Key Performance Indicators

## Total Patients
Counts the total number of unique patients.

Purpose:
Measures patient volume.

---

## Total Revenue
Sum of all collected payments.

Purpose:
Measures financial performance.

---

## Outstanding Payments

Purpose:
Shows unpaid balances requiring follow-up.

---

## Rejected Payments

Purpose:
Tracks insurance/payment failures.

---

## Average Satisfaction Score

Purpose:
Measures patient experience.

---

## Average Wait Time

Purpose:
Evaluates operational efficiency.
